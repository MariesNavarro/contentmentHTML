The Contentment Foundation believes in a world where happiness is cultivated from the inside out, not the other way around. We serve by helping children and adults draw their attention inward to experience the unconditional joy that has been there all along.

We offer philosophically and scientifically-evidenced curricula that cultivate mindfulness, compassion, gratitude, self-awareness, and other core approaches to human flourishing.

At the deepest core of human wellness is a sense of profound contentment, and uncovering it for yourself is your birthright.
