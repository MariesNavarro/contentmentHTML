Dave Kebo


Director of Storytelling



To me, “indestructible wellbeing” is a state of perfect balance within, for if you are in perfect balance within, then nothing outside can affect you. I believe all those who seek to realize this state of indestructible wellbeing have the power to inspire others to follow this path, thus exponentially spreading balance to the world. It must then be the goal and responsibility for all those who choose to walk this path to commit to help our fellow travelers realize their greatest selves and transform this world into a more compassionate one for all. I don’t claim to have realized this state — far from it. But I commit to walk the path. Dave is the visionary behind all of our visual media and is a master craftsman at telling powerful stories through film. He is currently working on short documentaries on the transformations we are witnessing at Four Pillars of Wellbeing Schools, and is the Manager of Video Production at the XPRIZE Foundation.
