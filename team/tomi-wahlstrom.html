Tomi Wahlström


Grant Writing Specialist



Tomi has focused on helping others realize sustainable wellbeing through his entire career since 1992. He began as a psychotherapist with focus on depth psychology, and since then he has worked at St. Petersburg College as a professor of management and personal development. Tomi is an advocate of transformational learning and believes that teaching others the most powerful way to create large-scale positive change. Tomi is leading the grant writing division at The Contentment Foundation and is working on several national and international collaborations to extend the reach of the Four Pillars of Wellbeing Curriculum. He is a powerful advocate for selfless service across all areas of work, a practice that he has seen offer powerful shifts in happiness over the course of his many travels and teaching experiences.
