Jessica Greenwalt


Graphic Designer & Head Parakeet



Jessica is a multiple award winning graphic designer, web developer, and illustrator who works on projects that seek to leave a lasting positive impact on the world. She’s one of Inc.'s "10 Women to Watch in Tech", Tech.co's "50 Women in Tech Dominating Silicon Valley", Huffington Post's "6 Women Rocking Tech for Good", and Today’s Leading Women's "Power 50". Jessica is the the founder and CEO of Pixelkeet, a graphic design firm based in Berkeley, California. Jessica is the mastermind behind all of the Contentment Foundation’s outer appearance, and she makes sure that all of our curricula, documents, and web offerings are visually stunning, easy to navigate, and conceptually simple.
