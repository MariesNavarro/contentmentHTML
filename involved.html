Get Involved


Donate

The easiest and fastest way to help us continue serving the world is by sending us a donation. As a registered 501(c)3 nonprofit foundation, your donation is tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. Let us know where you would like your donation to go (e.g., education, science, outreach) – and we’ll make sure that it’s used exactly for that purpose.

Volunteer, Research, and Affiliate

If you have some great skills that you’d like to dedicate toward our work, or if you’re a researcher looking to dig into the data, or if you’re an organization looking to co-promote – let us know by using the form below. We’d love to hear from you. The more we work together, the more amazing this world will be.
