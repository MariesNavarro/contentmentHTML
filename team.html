Our Team

The Contentment Foundation is powered by a team of international experts spanning across disciplines like psychology, education, philosophy, business, politics, art, social media, and science. We have come together to invite you into the radical perspective that fundamental wellbeing – contentment – already exists within you.


We are proud to work with a globally diverse team to turn our vision – promoting indestructible wellbeing worldwide – into a reality.
It is by understanding our diverse needs and perspectives, and finding the unifying principles behind them that make humanity truly great. We are honored to highlight our team here.
